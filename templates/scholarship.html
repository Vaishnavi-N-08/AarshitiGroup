Aarshiti Foundation Scholarships

‘We make a living by what we get but we make a life by what we give’
We at Arshiti group believe in giving our best to society! We strive towards financially helping some of the brightest minds of our State. The process of choosing these candidates will be purely merit-based. Three of the best and brightest students among all the applicants will be selected as beneficiaries of the scholarship of ₹10,000/- each. The Aarshiti Group aspires to encourage deserving and needful students with this scholarship scheme. It should be noted that only first year students are eligible for this scheme.

Required Documents

  • Income Certificate
  • Id Proof
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Fees Reciept

Eligibility Criteria

  • Annual Income should be less than 1,00,000 ₹
  • Nationality should be Indian
  • 12th grades should be more than 60%
  • He/She should be a student of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune
